ROHIT SHARMA

Rohit Sharma is the first player to score 5 T20I centuries.[4] He plays for Mumbai Indians in IP L and for Mumbai in domestic cricket. Sharma formerly captained Mumbai Indians and the team has won 5 titles in 2013, 2015, 2017, 2019 and 2020 under his leadership, making him the most successful captain in IPL history, sharing this record with MS Dhoni (5 title wins in IPL).

With India, Sharma was a member of the team that won the 2007 T20 World Cup.
