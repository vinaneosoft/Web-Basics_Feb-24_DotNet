CAKES

a sweet baked food made from a dough or thick batter usually containing flour and sugar and often shortening, eggs, and a raising agent

CUPCAKE

Cupcakes are small, tasty snack cakes that are favored for their portability and portion-control. They are batter cakes baked in a cup-shaped foil or temperature .

BROWNIE

A chocolate brownie, or simply a brownie, is a chocolate baked confection. Brownies come in a variety of forms and may be either fudgy or cakey, depending on their density.
