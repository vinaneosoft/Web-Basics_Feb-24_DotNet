About Us

EAT CLUB is dedicated to providing delicious and healthy meals for every occasion. Our mission is to make eating well easy and enjoyable for everyone. We believe that good food should be accessible to all, and we strive to create dishes that not only taste great but also nourish the body and soul. A cake is a sweetened baked food, usually made with flour, eggs, sugar, and a leavening agent. Shortening may or may not be used. Shortening is a fat or oil of animal or vegetable origin, such as butter, lard, vegetable oils, processed shortenings, and margarine.
