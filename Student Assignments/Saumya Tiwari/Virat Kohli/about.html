About Virat

  • Certainly, here's a concise overview of Virat Kohli's personal information and his journey to becoming a cricketer in 10 sentences:

  • Virat Kohli, born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, is a renowned Indian cricketer.

  • His father, Prem Kohli, worked as a criminal lawyer, and his mother, Saroj Kohli, is a housewife.

  • Kohli grew up in a middle-class family and attended Vishal Bharti Public School.

  • He began playing cricket at a young age and trained at the West Delhi Cricket Academy.

  • Kohli's talent was evident early on, and he captained the Indian Under-19 cricket team to victory in the 2008 ICC Under-19 Cricket World Cup.

  • His impressive performances at the U-19 level earned him a spot in the senior Indian cricket team.

  • Kohli made his international debut for India in August 2008 in an ODI against Sri Lanka.

  • Over the years, he has established himself as one of the best batsmen in the world, known for his aggressive style and consistent performances.

  • Kohli has broken numerous records and received several awards, including the prestigious ICC Cricketer of the Year multiple times.

  • Beyond cricket, Kohli is also recognized for his philanthropic work and is married to Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma.
